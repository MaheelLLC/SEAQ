About

Welcome to CRSD's Air Quality Portal! Dedicated to enhancing awareness about Dearborn's southern end air conditions, our platform offers valuable insights and resources. Here, you'll find comprehensive guides on reporting air quality violations and instructions for setting up air purifiers in your residence. Although our primary focus is the South End community, our mission is to enlighten all about the environmental challenges faced by this region.

With your support, positive change is on the horizon. We are excited to share that air purifiers will soon be a standard feature in every South End home. Our website aims to be your go-to resource for seamless purifier installation. Dive in, discover more, and see how you can contribute to this initiative. Thank you for investing your time in our endeavor!
