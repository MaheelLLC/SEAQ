Air Quality Violations

In the South End, if you see any of these bad physical signs, they could be air violations!

What are they?

  • Any Unclear, Colored, Excessive Smoke

    (Can be gray or any other color and must appear from any smoke stack or property)

  • Any Foul Odor Including Waste, Fire, or Chemical Smells

  • Any Developing Health Issues Such as Asthma

How Do I Report Them?

  • Phone:

  • Step 1: Call PEAS EGLE: (800) 292-4706

  • Step 2: State the location and description of air quality violation(s)

    (Location: South End, Dearborn and Factory Name/Location)

    (Description: Bad Physical Sign(s))

  • Online:

  • Step 1: Google Michigan Department of Air Quality

  • Step 2: Click on the link that says “Michigan Department of Air Quality<

    (Usually, it's the first link)

  • Step 3: Click “Submit a Violation”

  • Step 4: Fill out and submit the form

WHy Should I Report Them?

On April 11, 2023, a settlement between Cleveland Cliffs Inc. and PEAS EGLE arose over air quality violations by the company. Because of this settlement, our community will receive over $250,000 in funding. The Concerned Residents of South Dearborn will manage the funding to purchase an air purifier for every home in the South End. AQ violations brought this environmental project into fruition, so reporting these violations can help your community.
